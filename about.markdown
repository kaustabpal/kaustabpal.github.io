I am a research assistant at <a href="https://www.iiit.ac.in/">IIIT, Hyderabad</a> in the <a href="https://robotics.iiit.ac.in/">Robotics Research Center</a>, advised by <a href="https://faculty.iiit.ac.in/~mkrishna/">Dr. K. Madhava Krishna</a>. My work is focused mainly on the design and development of algorithms that enables autonomous vehicles to navigate safely in unstructured environments.

Previously I was associated with <a href="https://www.tcs.com/research-and-innovation">TCS Research & Innovation</a>, as part of the embedded robotics group under the guidance of Dr. Arijit Mukherjee and Mr. Sounak Dey. There I worked primarily on the applications of computational neuroscience algorithms in the field of robotics.

I did my undergrad in Electronics & Communications Engineering from UEM, Jaipur, where I worked on image processing applications like steganography in digital images and hand-gesture recognition algorithms to control non-holonomic robots.
