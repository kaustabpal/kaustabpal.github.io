I am a M.S. by research student in the <a href="https://robotics.iiit.ac.in/">Robotics Research Center</a> in <a href="https://www.iiit.ac.in/">IIIT, Hyderabad</a> under the guidance of <a href="https://faculty.iiit.ac.in/~mkrishna/">prof. Dr. K. Madhava Krishna</a>. 

Before joining <a href="https://www.iiit.ac.in/">IIIT, Hyderabad</a>, I was associated with <a href="https://www.tcs.com/research-and-innovation">TCS Research & Innovation</a>, where I was experimenting with the applications of computational neuroscience algorithms in the field of robotics.

I have started this blog with two main purpose in mind:

- I like learning about new concepts through experimentations. Through this blog, I intend to present my experimentation notes on all the topics that interests me.

- We don't really understand something unless we can explain it to a five year old. In this spirit, I intend to evaluate my understanding of different concepts by trying to explain them in the simplest ways possible.
