The vectors $$v_0, v_1, \dots, v_n$$ are said to have affine independence if the
vectors  $$v_1 - v_0, \dots, v_n - v_0$$ have [ [Linear Independence] ]( {%
post_url 2022-03-06-linear_independence %} ).
