# Sample space

A set of all possible outcomes. The sample space must be:

- Collectively exhaustive: The outcome of the expirement must be from inside the sample space.

- Mutually exclusive: For an experiment, only one possible outcome is possible from the sample space.

# Events

An event is a subset of a sample space. We find the probabilities of events occuring.
