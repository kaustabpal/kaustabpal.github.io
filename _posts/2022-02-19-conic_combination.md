Given a set of points $$x_1, x_2, \dots, x_k$$, the conic combination of these
points will be given as $$\theta_1 x_1 + \theta_2 x_2 + \dots + \theta_k x_k$$
where $$\theta_1, \theta_2, \dots, \theta_k \geq 0$$
