Given a set of $$k$$ points $$x_1, x_2, \dots x_k$$, the convex combination of
the points is a point of the form $$\theta_1 x_1 + \theta_2 x_2 + \dots + \theta_k
x_k$$ such that for $$i = 1 \dots k$$, $$\theta_i \geq 0$$ and $$\sum_{i=1}^{k}
\theta_i = 1$$.
