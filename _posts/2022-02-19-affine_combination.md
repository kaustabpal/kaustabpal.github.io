Given a set of points $$x_1, x_2, \dots, x_k$$, the affine combination of these
points is given by $$\theta_1 x_1 + \theta_2 x_2 \dots + \theta_k x_k$$ where
$$\sum_{i=1}^k \theta_i = 1$$
